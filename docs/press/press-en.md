## LinkedIn (professional)

**Title:** From AI-assisted team to autonomous software factory

Over the last few months we've built something unusual: a software team made entirely of specialized AI agents (PM, UX, Tech Lead, Frontend, Backend, QA, DevOps). Each with their own role, voice and judgment criteria.

Until today, we orchestrated them manually. Every handoff was a prompt. Every decision, a conversation.

Today we're publishing the BRD for the **Autonomous Orchestrator**: the system that will let them work end-to-end with no continuous human operator, mirroring the flow of a traditional software factory.

Lessons so far:

- Autonomy is not absence of control. It is **control at the right moments**.
- Gates matter more than agents.
- Token budget is the new efficiency metric.
- The human operator gains time, doesn't lose control.

Roadmap: MVP in 6 weeks. Hello World in 30 days. If you want the technical detail, BRD link in the comments.
